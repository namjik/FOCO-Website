ABOUT

This is the story of Ilsang.

Ilsang is a modern, casual, and simple-style cafe in Seoul, specifically located in Hongdae, from South Korea. We serve fresh basked desserts, tasty coffee, and other drinks.

The cafe name ‘Ilsang’ means daily and everyday life. Just like the name of our cafe, we would like everyone to enjoy and feel free to visit our cafe every day.

Ilsang cafe is very popular among young adults because of its dark-themed and chic aesthetic, and also of course its delicious desserts and drinks.
